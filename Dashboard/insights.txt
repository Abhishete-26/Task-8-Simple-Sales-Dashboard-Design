* Sales Dashboard Insights

1️⃣ West Region Generated the Highest Sales

The West region shows the highest total sales compared to East, Central, and South.
This indicates strong market performance and customer demand in the West zone.

2️⃣ Sales Peak Observed in Late 2017

Monthly trend shows the highest spike around November–December 2017 (~118K).
This suggests strong Q4 seasonal performance, likely due to holiday demand.

3️⃣ Technology Category Contributes the Most Revenue

Technology accounts for 36.4% of total sales, making it the top-performing category.
Furniture (32.3%) and Office Supplies (31.3%) follow closely.

4️⃣ Sales Show Fluctuating Monthly Pattern

Sales trend is not stable — it fluctuates between ~40K and 118K.
This suggests possible seasonality or promotional cycles affecting performance.
